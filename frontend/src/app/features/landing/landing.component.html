Does the AI actually sound human?

Yes — natural voice, interruptions, follow-up probes. Built on a production voice pipeline with low-latency speech-to-text and natural TTS.

Which languages are supported?

English, German, Spanish, Italian and Dutch. The voice agent runs in the language you pick at session start.

What happens to my CV?

Stored encrypted in EU infrastructure. Phone, address and email are stripped before any AI call. You can delete everything from your dashboard in one click.

How long are the sessions?

You pick. Five lengths: 15, 30, 45, 60 or 90 minutes. Shorter slots are great for a quick drill; longer slots mirror a full super-day round.
